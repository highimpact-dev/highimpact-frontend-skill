Rooted in Craft, Driven by Origin

Ember & Oak was born in a converted garage in Southeast Portland, where two friends discovered that great coffee isn't made -- it's revealed. Every bean carries the fingerprint of its soil, its altitude, its climate. Our job is simply to listen.

We travel to origin, building direct relationships with farmers who share our obsession with quality. From the highlands of Ethiopia to the volcanic slopes of Guatemala, we seek out lots that tell a story in every cup.

Each batch is roasted by hand in our 15-kilo Probat, profiled to bring out the character that makes single-origin coffee extraordinary. No blending. No shortcuts. Just honest coffee.

From Seed to Cup

Every bag of Ember & Oak coffee follows a deliberate path from farm to your morning ritual.

01

Source

We visit farms personally, cupping hundreds of lots each season to find beans with exceptional character and complexity.

02
